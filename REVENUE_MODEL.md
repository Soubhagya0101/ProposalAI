# ProposalAI Revenue Model

## Offer

ProposalAI helps new freelancers turn a job description into a ready-to-send proposal in their own voice in under 30 seconds.

## First Revenue Path

- Free early access for new freelancers with fewer than 10 reviews.
- Ask for feedback after first use.
- Convert warm users to a simple $9/month plan.
- Optional done-for-you profile setup at $49 for freelancers who want help writing their profile once.

## Funnel

1. Lead Hunter imports approved Upwork/API/export leads with fewer than 10 reviews.
2. Outreach Writer creates a personalized message under 100 words.
3. Founder reviews and sends inside the allowed channel.
4. Follow Up Manager drafts one follow-up after 3 days if there is no response.
5. Feedback Collector asks:
   - Did the proposal sound like you?
   - What would make it better?
   - Would you pay $9/month for this?
6. Revenue Tracker reports leads, sends, replies, free users, paying users, and conversion rates daily.

## Safety Rule

Do not scrape Upwork or auto-send cold messages. Use official API access, user-provided exports, or manual review. The system intentionally drafts and tracks outreach instead of sending spam.
